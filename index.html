Deep Water Properties for BC Inlets

Below are the time series of deep sea temperature (°C), salinity (PSU), and dissolved oxygen (mL/L) for a selection of inlets in BC. Click on the tabs to view plots.

The time series are coloured by depth category, which is different for each inlet. Each time series displays how depths are sorted.

Data is taken from the archive at waterproperties.ca and Hakai Water Properties Vertical Profile Data Measures by Oceanographic Profilers, Provisional. This is a mix of netCDF, IOS Shell, and CSV files.

Click here to view map

Data is averaged inside individual depth buckets across a month. This produces a maximum of three data points per month, one for each bucket. The average is calculated as the arithmetic mean of all data in a single depth category across a month.

Data is not averaged. All data found is shown, coloured according to its depth.

Data is averaged annually and presented in a single plot for each data value. Annomalies (deviation from the average) are calculated and plotted for each data value as well. All inlets are shown in the same plot. The average is calculated as the arithmetic mean of all data considered deep across a month.
